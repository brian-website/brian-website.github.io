Welcome!

This is a demonstration of sections appearing as you scroll. The effect is subtle and enhances user experience by drawing attention to content as it comes into view.

Why Use Scroll Animations?

Scroll-triggered animations can make websites feel more interactive and modern, guiding the visitor’s attention and making the experience more engaging.

How Does This Work?

This example uses a small amount of JavaScript to detect when a section enters the viewport, then adds a CSS class to trigger the fade-in animation.

Mobile Friendly

The layout is fully responsive, so it looks great on both desktop and mobile devices. Try resizing the browser or viewing on your phone!

Get Started!

You can use this technique on your own sites to highlight features, products, or any important content. It’s lightweight and easy to implement!
