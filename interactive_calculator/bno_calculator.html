FAQs

Do I need to add a day trip? +

No. If you left the UK and returned on the same day (for example, left on 01/09/2024 and re‑entered on 01/09/2024), this does not count as any days outside the UK. The calculator only counts full days when you were physically out of the country.

Will my results be saved on a server other than my device? +

No. This is an open‑source project with no database or external storage. All calculations happen locally on your device. No data is shared, stored, or accessed by external services -- your results remain completely private to you.

How does this system calculate the result? +

This calculator follows the current BNO visa rules.

Settlement eligibility: You must not have spent more than 180 days outside the UK in any rolling 12‑month period during the 5 years before your settlement application.

Citizenship eligibility: You must not have spent more than 450 days outside the UK in total during the 5 years before your application, and no more than 90 days outside the UK in the final 12 months.

The calculator applies both sets of requirements together and determines the earliest possible date you could become eligible for citizenship.
